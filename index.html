About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on 23 September 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 organization committed to building an inclusive and empowered society through sustainable initiatives.


The foundation is recognized by NGO Darpan (NITI Aayog) and is ISO 9001:2015 certified. It works in multiple sectors including education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare and social welfare.
